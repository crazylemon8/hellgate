## Overview

Hellgate is an offline mobile game. At the time of this policy, Hellgate does not require account creation, does not provide online multiplayer, and does not use advertising, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Hellgate handles, how that data is used, and your choices.

## Information We Process

### Data stored locally on your device

Hellgate stores a small amount of gameplay and settings data locally on your device so the game can function properly. This may include:

- whether you have completed the tutorial
- your audio settings, such as music and SFX preferences
- your saved high score

This information is stored locally in the app's save data and is not transmitted to us by the game.

### Data we do not currently collect

At this time, Hellgate does not collect or transmit personal data such as:

- your name
- your email address
- your phone number
- your precise location
- your contacts
- your photos or media library
- your payment information
- account credentials
- advertising identifiers
- analytics or gameplay telemetry sent to our servers

Hellgate also does not currently operate its own game servers or online account system.

## How We Use Data

The local data stored by Hellgate is used only to support gameplay and app preferences, including:

- remembering whether the tutorial has already been completed
- remembering music and sound settings
- remembering the highest score achieved on the device

## Sharing of Data

Hellgate does not currently share your in-game local save data with us or with third parties through the app.

If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when those changes take effect.

## Retention and Deletion

Hellgate stores its save data locally on your device until you remove the app, clear the app's data, or delete the relevant saved data from the device.

Because this data is currently stored locally and not uploaded to our servers by the game, you can generally remove it by:

- uninstalling the app
- clearing app storage on your device
- deleting the game's local save data where available

## Children's Privacy

Hellgate does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Security

Because Hellgate currently stores only local gameplay and settings data on the device, security depends largely on your device environment and operating system protections. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the game is designed to minimize data handling.

## Third-Party Links

If Hellgate later includes links to external websites or storefront pages, those destinations may have their own privacy practices. Please review those policies separately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the game, legal requirements, or data practices. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
