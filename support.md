## How to Play

**Goal:** Sort skeletons into the correct gate before they reach the end of the lane.
- Red skeletons → push them **left**
- Green skeletons → push them **right**

**Controls (mobile):**
- Joystick (bottom-right) — move left/right
- Sprint button (bottom-left) — hold to run faster
- Tap joystick up — jump
- Jump + sprint — combo for long distance

**Controls (keyboard):**
- A / D — move
- Space — jump
- Shift — sprint
- P — pause

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The game isn't loading. What should I do?**
Force-quit the app and reopen it. If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling.

**Q: My progress/settings were reset.**
Tutorial completion, audio preferences, and high score are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app clears this data. iCloud backup does not apply.

**Q: The audio isn't working.**
Check that your device is not in silent mode. In-game audio can also be toggled from the pause screen (tap the pause button during gameplay).

**Q: The game feels laggy or stutters.**
Close background apps and ensure your device has sufficient free storage. Hellgate is designed to run smoothly on modern iOS devices.

**Q: How do I pause the game?**
Tap the pause button in the top-right corner of the screen during gameplay.

**Q: Is there an internet connection required?**
No. Hellgate is fully offline. No account, login, or internet access is needed.

**Q: Does the game collect any personal data?**
No. The only data stored is local to your device: tutorial completion status, audio preferences, and your high score. Nothing is transmitted or shared. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for full details.
